Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Examivo ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your information when you use the Examivo mobile application and related services.

By using Examivo, you agree to the collection and use of information according to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile details, login credentials, and account information provided during registration.
  • Educational Data: Quiz results, mock test scores, learning progress, subject preferences, bookmarks, and study activity.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, crash logs, app version, unique device identifiers, IP address, and diagnostic data.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage information such as app interactions, session duration, visited screens, feature usage, and performance analytics.
  • Subscription & Billing Data: Purchase status and subscription information processed securely through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To provide educational services and mock tests.
  • To track learning progress and improve user experience.
  • To personalize app content and recommendations.
  • To improve app performance, security, and reliability.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized activity.
  • To process subscriptions and premium features.
  • To respond to support requests and user inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services that help operate and improve Examivo. These providers may collect limited information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, database storage, analytics, crash reporting, and app security.
  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data for personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • RevenueCat: Used for secure subscription validation and purchase management.
  • Google Play Billing: Used for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions securely.

4. Advertising Policy

Examivo may display ads to support free access to educational content. Advertisements may be personalized based on your device and app activity.

Users can manage ad personalization preferences through their device settings or Google account settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Secure Firebase infrastructure
  • Encrypted network communication (HTTPS)
  • Firebase App Check and authentication security
  • Restricted admin access controls
  • Subscription validation security

However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Account Deletion

Users have full control over their accounts.

You can permanently delete your account and associated data directly from the app by navigating to:

Settings → Delete Account

Once deleted, your account information, test history, preferences, and associated data may be permanently removed from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

Examivo is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such data promptly.

9. User Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Update or correct account information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Request information about stored data.
  • Manage advertisement personalization preferences.
